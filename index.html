About Me

I am a versatile Software Engineer with a deep-rooted expertise in Linux environments and a passion for architecting robust Infrastructure as Code. While many focus solely on the code, I take immense pride in understanding the full scope of computing—from the hardware and virtualization layers (I'm a Proxmox enthusiast) to the high-level financial APIs I orchestrate daily. This "full-stack" perspective allows me to build systems that aren't just functional, but truly resilient and performant.

Personally, I'm a father of two living in Austin, though I frequently split my time on my family's ranch in Abilene. When I'm not deep in a terminal or chasing around kids, I'll likely find me outdoors on the water, working on a hands-on project, or lost in music. Between art and engineering, I've always been driven by the love of creating something meaningful.
